I am a PG design student specializing in Interaction Design from Industrial Design Centre - IIT Bombay. My UG specialisation was in Electronics and Media Technology.

As a designer, I am passionate about getting to the heart of a need or a problem and then bringing together the best of technology and human capabilities for a solution. Conducting user studies, wireframing, sketching user experiences and rapid prototyping are some of my strong skills. When I'm not doing any of these, I go around hunting dragons, chasing comets on an inter-galatic race and excavating dead pharoahs out of their tombs. But if you're really interested I could join you in playing guitar, gardening and painting or just a conversation over a hot cup of coffee. :D

Feel free to get in touch if you have a need/problem that demands the attention of an interaction designer.
